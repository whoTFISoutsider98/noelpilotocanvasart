Biography

Born in 1969 and raised in Cuba, Noel Piloto has always had a deep passion for the arts. His journey began in the vibrant streets of Havana, where the rich culture and history inspired his early works. Moving to Miami, Florida, he continued to pursue his love for painting, creating pieces that reflect both his Cuban heritage and his experiences in the United States.
